Verifiable Credentials and Selective Disclosure

This protocol supports verifiable credentials and verifiable presentations according to [[vc-data-model]]. Additionally it supports a special way implementing the selective disclosure-feature. This feature gives the credential-holder the oppurtunity, to choose which attributes to show to the verifier. To achive this every attribute-value a credential contains is hashed with a salt. The final verifiable credential will only contain the hashes; the salts and values are stored in a separate json-object, called plaintext-credential. The following example will show such a plaintext-credential and its corresponding verifiable credential.
